Meaty Spaghetti

This simple dish will surely be a hit with the family. It combines the flavors of ground pork with the rich flavor of tomatoes and cheese.

30 MINS Cooking Time
Easy Difficulty
6 People Serves

Ingredients

  • 400 grams spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 pc onion
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • ½ kg ground pork
  • 2 pcs Knorr pork or beef broth cubes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 500 grams tomato sauce
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • Grated cheese for topping

Filter recipes by...

Chicken Pork Beef Quick & Easy

Cook

1
First, cook the spaghetti noodles according to package directions. Drain well and set aside.
2
Next, get a pan and make it nice and hot over medium heat. Sauté onions and garlic until light brown. Add ground pork and cook until the color changes.
3
Add Knorr Pork Cubes, stir well to dissolve before adding the tomato paste and tomato sauce. Sauté for 2 minutes.
4
Add sugar, pepper and water and simmer for 15-20 minutes until the sauce has thickened.
5
Pour the sauce over your cooked spaghetti noodles and sprinkle with plenty of grated cheese. Enjoy!
