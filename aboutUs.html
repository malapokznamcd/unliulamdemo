About TechBoys

Creative minds united by technology, innovation, and collaboration.

TechBoys is a dynamic group of passionate students from the University of Caloocan City Congress Campus, dedicated to exploring web development, design, and the power of digital technology.

Our goal is to create meaningful and user-friendly digital projects that bring together creativity and practicality. Each member contributes unique skills — from front-end design to logic building — making TechBoys a well-balanced and innovative team.
